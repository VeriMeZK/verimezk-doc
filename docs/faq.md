# Frequently Asked Questions

### Is VeriMeZK free to use?

Yes. VeriMeZK is open-source and distributed under the MIT license.

---

### Does it store any personal data?

No. All processing occurs locally in the browser, so nothing is stored or transmitted.

---

### Can I use it without a blockchain?

Yes. Proofs can be verified entirely off-chain when on-chain validation is not required.

---

### What if the face scan fails?

The user is prompted to retry the capture. Good lighting and camera quality improve reliability.

---

### Can I define my own rules?

Yes. See [Custom Checks](./features/custom-checks.md) for guidance on composing new rules.
